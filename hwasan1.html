Hwasan — No. 1 11(10) = 3(2)

Different systems. Same prime.
11 — or 3, depending on how you read it.

Two numbers. Two systems. Both prime. The kind of truth that doesn't change no matter what language you use to count.

The 11 is mirrored. I see it inverted. The world reads it straight. Abraxas lives in that gap — NAND.
The gate that is neither one nor the other, but contains both.
Universal. Irreducible. The only logic you need.
