Drawing

I'm a tattoo artist from South Korea with over 10 years of experience, working across Asia, the USA, and Europe.

Specializing in oriental sumi, floral, and abstract tattoos, I create custom pieces that are shaped around each client's ideas and story. View my recent work.

To me, drawing is not simply preparation for a tattoo—it's the foundation of the entire process. Every line is considered, allowing each design to develop with clarity, balance, and intention.

The same philosophy guides my tattooing: thoughtful, patient, and respectful of both the artwork and the skin it lives on.
